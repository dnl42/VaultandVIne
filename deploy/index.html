  • Our Story

Vault + Vine is a floral studio and thoughtfully curated retail space in East Falls, Philadelphia. Founded in 2017, we offer:

Floral design services for weddings and events; Custom floral arrangements made daily and delivered; Plants for the home and garden; A collection of specialty homegoods, gifts, and objects (many sourced from Philadelphia-area creators); A café and gathering place. At the heart of our shop is love for our community and a desire to create, promote, and connect with our neighbors. Our vision is to create a community of businesses and customers that supports, promotes, and celebrates the local and global economy and bydemanding accountability in every aspect of the supply chain.
